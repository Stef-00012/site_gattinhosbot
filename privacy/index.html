Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We may collect and store the following information:

  • Your Discord server ID: we may collect and store your Discord server ID in order to store the bot's data of the server such as reaction roles data, welcome messages data or any server-specific feature.
  • Your Discord user ID and username: we may collect and store you Discord ID to store your leveling data for each server you're in. We may collect your Discord username in order to show your username in the /leaderboard command.
  • Your Discord user avatar and banner URLs: we may collect and store your Discord avatar and banner URLs to store a permanent URL which is used in the logs system.
  • The URLs of any image that can be used in the logs.
  • Any data submitted through slash commands, buttons, select menus and modals: we may collect and store the any data submitted through slash commands, buttons, select menus and modals used for any server-specific feature.

The bot also sends anything done with it (slash commands executed, buttons pressed, used select menus and modals submitted along with the modal data) to a private discord channels accessible only by the developers.

If you want to opt-out this kind of logging or want to opt-out the images (Discord user avatar and banner, Discord server icon and banner, emojis, stickers, event's covers, role icons etc.) collection, you can opt-out by pressing the "Opt-Out" button in the /privacy command. If you have the "Administrator" permission you will have the choice to opt-out just for yourself as user or for the whole server.
